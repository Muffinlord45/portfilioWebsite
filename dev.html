Hestia Power PCB

2S LiPo based power distribution PCB with undervoltage lockout and overcurrent protection.

July. 2024 - Aug. 2024

Demo Video 
Summary

Hestia was born from the need to have a robust power management solution. In previous years, we would use LiPo packs wired directly into our electronics. This caused headaches for charging, undervolting the packs, and if there were shorts it could potentially catch the batteries on fire from unregulated current draw.

To solve this, we designed Hestia, named after the greek goddess of hearth and home, somewhere that you'd metaphorically "recharge" :). Hestia features a robust undervoltage lockout with 200mV of hysteresis using an op-amp based design to combat the over-discharging of the batteries. It also has a resettable fuse for overcurrent incidents. At room temp this would be 10A, but we're operating in conditions of 50C, where the fuse trips at 7.8A instead.
